Simpang Lima Gumul

The Simpang Lima Gumul Monument or commonly abbreviated as SLG is one of the buildings that has become an icon of Kediri Regency which resembles the Arc de Triomphe in Paris, France. The SLG was built in 2003 and inaugurated in 2008, which was initiated by the then Regent of Kediri, Sutrisno. This building is located in Tugurejo Village, Ngasem District, Kediri Regency, East Java Province, precisely at the center of the meeting of the five roads leading to the City of Kediri, Pagu, Pare, Pesantren and Plosoklaten, Kediri

Air Terjun Dolo

Dolo Waterfall is one of the waterfall attractions located in Besuki Hamlet, Jugo Village, Mojo District, Kediri Regency, East Java. This waterfall is located on the eastern slope of Mount Wilis , which has a height of 125 meters. The water spill that falls from above is divided into three parts, starting from the highest part of about 90 meters and below about 2-5 meters. Dolo waterfall is located in a mountainous area, so in addition to the cold air temperature, often the area around the waterfall and the access road to get there is covered by fog

Goa Selomangleng

Selomangleng Cave is located at Jalan Mastrip, Kediri. Formerly this cave was the hermitage of Dewi Kilisuci, the daughter of the Kahuripan Kingdom led by King Airlangga. According to the story, the princess decided to meditate to ask for prosperity for the people of Kediri. He then decided not to marry and leave the world
